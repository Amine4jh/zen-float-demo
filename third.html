Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring?

Just yourself. We provide everything you need including towels, earplugs, organic body wash, shampoo, and post-float amenities like hairdryers and moisturizer. Most people choose to float in the nude for the ultimate sensory deprivation experience, as swimsuits can create distracting tactile sensations.

What if I'm claustrophobic?

Our modern float pods are extremely spacious—the size of a small car interior. You have complete control over your environment. You can leave the pod door open, keep the interior soft LED lights on, or play relaxing music until you feel comfortable exploring total darkness and silence.

Is the water completely clean?

Absolutely. The exceptionally high concentration of Epsom salt creates a highly sterile environment. Additionally, our state-of-the-art filtration system cycles the entire volume of water through a 1-micron filter, UV light, and natural oxidization processes 4 times between every single float session.
