The Float Experience

Disconnect to Reconnect

Floating, or Restricted Environmental Stimulation Therapy (REST), provides a highly unique environment free from external sensory input.

Suspended in 10 inches of water saturated with 1,000 lbs of Epsom salt, your body floats effortlessly. As the water is heated exactly to skin temperature, the boundary between your body and the water slowly disappears, leaving you in a state of profound peace and physical recovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my float session?
Just bring yourself! We provide everything you need including towels, earplugs, premium organic body wash, and post-float tea. You float completely nude to remove all tactile sensations, so no swimsuit is required.
Will I feel claustrophobic?
It's highly unlikely. Our tanks are very spacious. You are always in complete control of your environment—you can leave the gentle interior light on, play soft ambient music, or even leave the tank door slightly open if you prefer.
How is the water kept clean?
The heavy concentration of medical-grade Epsom salt makes the water inherently sterile. Furthermore, between every single session, the entire volume of water is cycled three times through an industrial filtration system utilizing UV light, ozone purification, and a 1-micron filter.
