Frequently Asked

Absolutely. The water in our float pods is only 25–30 cm deep, and the ultra-high concentration of Epsom salt (approximately 550 kg per pod) means your body floats effortlessly on the surface — no swimming ability required. You stay completely in control and can sit up or exit at any time.
Avoid caffeine 4–6 hours before your session. Don't shave or wax within 24 hours as the salt can sting. Eat a light meal about an hour prior — not too full, not too hungry. We provide earplugs, a neck float pillow, and a thorough pre-session briefing. All you need to bring is yourself.
Our filtration system cycles the entire volume of salt water through a 1-micron filter between every session. We use UV sterilisation and a minimum concentration of hydrogen peroxide — far safer and gentler than chlorine. The extreme salinity also creates a naturally hostile environment for bacteria. Our hygiene protocols are RIVM-certified.
Many pregnant women find floating deeply relieving — especially in the second and third trimesters when the weightlessness takes pressure off the spine and joints. We recommend consulting your midwife or GP first. We do ask that you avoid floating in the first trimester out of precaution.
